Korvstroganoff

Korvstroganoff

Korvstroganoff is a Swedish delicacy. It is made from sausages.

Ingredients

  1. 4 rice or other grains....................
  2. 1 yellow onion..............................
  3. 1 tablespoon oil.................................
  4. 3 tablespoons tomato puree...............
  5. 2 1/2 dl cooking cream..........
  6. 1 dl milk>...............................
  7. 1 tablespoon Japanese soy...........
  8. 1 tsp Dijon mustard........................
  9. pepper................................................
  10. salt.................................................
Ingredients for seasoning and serving
coarsely chopped white cabbage with parsley

Preparation

  1. Cook the rice according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Cut the sausage into sticks. Peel and chop the onion.
  3. Fry sausage and onion in the oil in a frying pan for about 5 minutes. Add tomato puree and fry for a minute.
  4. Stir in cream, milk, soy and dijon mustard. Let simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Season with pepper and possibly salt.
  6. o serve: Serve the sausage stroganoff with rice and preferably cabbage salad.
